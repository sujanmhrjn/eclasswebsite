Art Competition

Students will participate in weekly art competitions, on particular theme that are connected to their curriculum. They can access various eLearning videos related to Art and hone their skills. A winner from each class and school is selected on a weekly basis by the judges of Midas eClass. The winners will progress to the physical edition and the national winner will be awarded ‘The Next Picasso’ title for the year.
